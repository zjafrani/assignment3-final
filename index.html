About Me

Hello, My name is Zain and I am a 2nd Year Student at The University Of Waterloo. I am going to be Studying Global Business and Digital Arts. Some of my interests include, Film, Digital Media, UI/UX design, Photography, Videography and more

Portfolio and Projects

During my time in the GBDA propgram at the University of Waterloo, I've had many opportunities to express my creativity. One of my favourite classes during my time in GBDA so far was GBDA 201: Introduction to Digital Media. One of my favourite projects that we worked on during this class was the 1st project, our photography project. This Project gave me the opportunity to express my creativity through one of my favourite mediums, Photography and truly gave me free reign to do whatever when it came to taking these photos. Another project that I thoroughly enjoyed creating was the short, silent film that we made for our 2nd project.

Watch it here: SLC Marketplace: A Silent Film.

Both of these projects were not only fun and entertaining to create but allowed for me to express my creativity through different mediums but also allowed me to learn and gain new and practical skills for future projects and potential careers.
